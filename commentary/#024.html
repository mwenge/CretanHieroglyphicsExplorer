Comments
a. The signgroup can be read in six different ways, two of which (simple orthograde and simple retrograde readings) have the same Linear A parallel.
g: Notes to Signgroups XI, suggests, on the analogy of HT 30, that 011-056 is an abbreviation for < > 011 056<070>. The similiarity here with #015.g suggests that the impression in a1 may be similar to 049-070-070 RO 3 -RO-RO #015.a1; cf. the impression here with the more conventional impressions on the similar crescents #015 and #026.
